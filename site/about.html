About us

We grew up in small business. So did Gritty Bee.

We started Gritty Bee because we watched too many great local businesses — the kind with a line out the door on a good night — struggle to be found on a slow one.

The product was never the problem. Being seen was.

Our founders spent years in and around independent businesses — working the counter, managing the floor, and later building marketing for the big chains. They kept noticing the same thing: the chains had entire teams making them look effortless online, while the best little spots in the neighbourhood were posting a blurry photo once a month, if at all.

It wasn't a talent gap. It was a resources gap. The independent places had the grit and the regulars — but none of the marketing muscle that turns that into a full house, week after week.

So we built a hive for hire. One flat fee, every channel handled, one approval a month. The marketing team the big guys take for granted, finally sized for the shop on the corner.
