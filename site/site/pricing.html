Questions

The honest answers.

Is there a contract?
No. Gritty Bee is month-to-month. Cancel any time with 30 days' notice, and you keep everything we've built — your profiles, your content, your website.
Does ad spend come out of the monthly fee?
No. Your Growth fee covers our management of your ads. Whatever you choose to spend on Google or Meta is billed to you directly by those platforms, so there's never a markup.
What do you need from me each month?
About thirty minutes. We send the month's plan, you approve it on one short call, and we handle the rest. If something urgent comes up — a sold-out event, a new dish — just text us.
How fast can we start?
Most businesses are fully live within five business days of the discovery call. The free audit happens before that, and there's no commitment to book it.
What if I already have a website or social accounts?
Even better. We'll audit what you have, keep what's working, and grow from there. You always own your accounts and your domain.
